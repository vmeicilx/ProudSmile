Welcome to Proud Smile

(formerly Gold Coast Dental Services)




As a valued patient from Gold Coast Dental Services we'd like to extend a special welcome to you from all of us at Proud Smile. In fact, you'll be happy to know that your favourite faces are still here like our lovely Maree and our long standing dentists Dr. Rebecca Bull and Dr. David Ng. Our team has grown now to include Dr. Cristine Volpato and Dr. Erwin Vasconcelos, the new owners, and our new Dental Hygienist/Therapist Natasha Satti. 

We have recently completed renovations to create a more inspiring place, more comfortable and more patient driven. Our vision is to redefine the concept of dentistry and to create a space where you'll love to come. 

You'll be glad to know that all the great things from GCDS have remained such as our strong relationship with Medibank Private as a preferred provider as well as Bupa Members First and HCF. Rest assured we have not increased any of our prices and in fact some items have reduced in cost such as our popular Zoom whitening now $495 (from $750), plus we now offer a comprehensive range of payment plans as we believe good quality dentistry should be accessible to all. 

In addition to our basic range of services we now offer state-of-the-art digital dentistry which means faster appointments and cleaner visits. We can now do crowns and other restorations in a single visit while you shop at Pac Fair. We have also introduced an exciting new aesthetic department where you'll access bespoke aesthetic treatments such as porcelain veneers, implants, full mouth rehabilitations and Invisalign Clear Aligners (we're a Platinum Provider). 

So, if you haven't yet seen your new clinic come and visit us, you'll be pleasantly surprised. You can get more information about us on our website and social media channels.
