Relax, you’re at Proud Smile

We understand that for many people, a simple trip to the dentist can be cause for extreme nervousness or fear.That is why at Proud Smile we take a different approach and we’ll try to make you overcome your fears. For starters we’re not a dental practice as you know it. We don’t look like one, we don’t smell like one. Even our clinical rooms are not sterile and cold.

We use LED ambiance lighting to create just the right mood. Watch Netflix while receiving your treatment and make use of our noise cancelling headphones. Blankets are available if you feel cold. Technology helps us comfort you and allows us to make your visits faster and smoother. Our caring team will take the time to talk with you, there’s no rushing.
