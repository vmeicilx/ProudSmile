Relax, you are at Proud Smile

We understand that for many people, a simple trip to the dentist can be cause for extreme nervousness or fear. At Proud Smile we take a different approach to help you overcome your fear. For starters we are not a dental practice as you know it. We don’t look like one, we don’t smell like one. Even our clinical rooms are not sterile and cold.

We use LED ambiance lighting to create just the right mood. Watch Netflix while receiving your treatment and make use of our noise cancelling headphones. Blankets are available if you feel cold. Technology helps us comfort you and allows us to make your visits faster and smoother. Our caring team will take the time to talk with you, there’s no rushing.

Advanced
Anaesthesia

At Proud Smile we are very serious about our patients feeling no pain from our procedures. We research the market constantly to find the latest advancements in anaesthesia technology and as such we have recently invested in an electric device called Dentapen. One of the main reasons for painful injections is the speed at which the anaesthetic is administered. With Dentapen the liquid is injected at a very slow speed, literally drops at a time. That single factor makes these injections a lot more tolerable and in some cases completely pain free.

Our local anaesthetics are of high potency and we apply advanced techniques so you won’t even feel a thing depending on the location in the mouth.

Sedation

For some of our patients dealing with their anxiety is just too difficult and beyond their control. Apart from all our amenities to make you feel relaxed we can also help with medication which makes you more relaxed and helps with analgesia. Subject to checking your medical history we can provide you with Penthrox, which is commonly known as the “Green Whistle”. This is used by paramedics to help calm down victims of accidents and help with their pain. In certain cases we can also prescribe mild sedatives to help with more invasive procedures such as surgeries.
