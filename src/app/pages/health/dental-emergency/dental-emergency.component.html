Get Help Right Now

If you find yourself with a dental emergency suffering from a serious dental condition such as a broken front tooth, an abscess, sporting injury or severe pain there’s only one thing you need and that is to be seen Right Now. So follow these easy 3 steps to get help now:

Book an appointment online now or call us on the numbers below if you can’t find availability. We reserve emergency appointments on most days and we will do our best to see you straight away. If your emergency relates to a serious accident or you are having difficulty in breathing please look for your closest hospital or call an ambulance.

Follow these tips according to the emergency you are experiencing, until you arrive at our practice:

TOOTH ACHE

If you haven’t already, take a pain killer that you have ready at home and that you usually take for a headache as soon as you’ve made your appointment.

BROKEN OR LOST TOOTH

Save the piece that’s broken. If the entire tooth came out place it in milk and bring it with you.

LOST CROWN

It is important to bring the crown with you to your appointment. You can use clove oil to help ease any pain or discomfort to your gums. DO NOT use super glue or other sticky substances to glue the crown back on.

YOU ARE BLEEDING

If you have a cut in your mouth grab a handkerchief and bite hard on it. It may also help to put a cold bag on your face on where you are bruised.

ABSCESS OR PUS

If you have a yellow pus coming from your gums or tooth, just rinse your mouth with warm water, don’t do anything to the tooth or gums and don’t attempt to drain the abscess, we will take care of that for you once you arrive.
