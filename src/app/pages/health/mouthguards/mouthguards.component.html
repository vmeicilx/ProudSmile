Teeth are precious,
why risk them?

A mouthguard is a coloured rubber device that fits over your teeth to protect them from any type of impact. If you or your child plays sport, a mouthguard may be a compulsory requirement before stepping up to play. If a mouthguard is not compulsory, you should still consider whether the use of one would be in the best interests of you and your family’s health and safety. We see too many broken and missing teeth from sports that are not considered to involve contact, such as tennis or soccer and we would rather help you protect your teeth than go through the pain and expense associated with a tooth that has been knocked out.

A boil-and-bite type mouthguard is just not enough, it doesn’t have enough retention and it has uneven thicknesses. We produce custom made mouthguard that will fit your teeth snugly. Why save on a mouthguard and spend more later on when you get an accident? It’s just not worth it. Plus we use digital scans, no moulds are necessary anymore.
