A mouthguard is a coloured rubber device that fits over your teeth to protect them from any type of impact. If you or your children play sports a mouthguard may be a compulsory requirement before stepping up to play. If a mouthguard is not compulsory, you should still consider whether the use of one would be in the best interests of you and your family’s health and safety. We see too many broken and missing teeth from sports that are not considered to involve contact, such as tennis or soccer and we would rather help you protect your teeth than go through the pain and expense associated with a tooth that has been knocked out.

A boil-and-bite type mouthguard is just not enough, it doesn’t have enough retention and it has uneven thicknesses. We produce custom made mouthguard that will fit your teeth snugly. Why save on a mouthguard and spend more later on when you get an accident? It’s just not worth it. Plus we use digital scans, no moulds are necessary anymore.

Bite Splints

If you grind or clench your teeth together you have a condition called Bruxism. If untreated this can cause mild to severe pain in the jaw and teeth, and can even cause headaches. Bruxism can cause wear and tear up to 80 times more than normal on the teeth, and it’s important to protect the teeth from damage along with reducing the unpleasant symptoms associated to this problem.

A Bite Splint is a dental device typically made of acrylic that covers your teeth in one of the arches and avoid them to touch. Benefits of bite splints include:

Protection of teeth and dental restorations from wear or breakage

Reducing biting forces on dental implants

Relief of certain types of muscle or joint pain

Prevent unwanted movement or shifting of teeth

Increased comfort

It’s an extremely simple process, we take digital scans of both your arches and it’s done, it’s literally 5 minutes. With the scans we then produce a milled splint out of a disc made of acrylic. The recommendation is to wear them every night but in more severe cases of grinding it’s a good idea to wear them during the day also.
