Supporting our Veterans

We’re proud to provide our services for holders of Gold and White cards from the Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA). You can receive the same high quality and utmost care and pay nothing for it as we bulk bill directly with the Department of Human Services.

What is covered
under DVA?

DVA covers most dental procedures and the great majority of them require no approval. There is an annual monetary limit which is not cumulative so the ideal is for patients to come regularly for dental examinations to assess for any required dental work.

The annual limit usually allows for 2 crowns per year so it’s a good idea to upgrade the large fillings into porcelain restorations and protect your teeth from fractures. Dental Implants require prior approval due to the complex nature of them and we’re happy to submit any required documentation to support your application.

For treatment plans amounting to more than the annual monetary limits we can offer a tailored payment plan to help with the excess costs, however these are not common as DVA does cover a wide range of dental procedures. Claims are done electronically so there are no forms to fill out. You receive treatment and the rest is all with us.
