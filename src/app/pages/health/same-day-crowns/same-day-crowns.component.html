Crowns

Beauty made strong

A dental crown is a “cap” made of porcelain or zirconia that mimics the same shape and colour of a natural tooth. Crowns are recommended when damage or extensive dental work makes a traditional resin filling unusable or too weak and the tooth then needs to be covered with a cap.

Crowns are custom-made from high-grade dental ceramic or zirconia which makes them a strong, long-lasting restoration that offer great aesthetic and long-term protection. Because these types of restorations are made outside the mouth they’re subject to stricter quality control since they’re not exposed to variables such as saliva, blood, neighbouring teeth, compared to a normal composite filling.

That also means you won’t need to be with your mouth opened for too long as the majority of the work is being handled in our lab while you wait.
