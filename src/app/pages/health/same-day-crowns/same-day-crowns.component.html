Crowns

Beauty made strong

A dental crown is a “cap” made of porcelain or zirconia that mimics the same shape and colour of a natural tooth. Crowns are recommended when damage or extensive dental work makes a traditional resin filling unusable or too weak and the tooth then needs to be covered with a cap.

Crowns are custom-made from high-grade dental ceramic or zirconia which makes them a strong, long-lasting restoration that offer great aesthetic and long-term protection. Because these types of restorations are made outside the mouth they’re subject to stricter quality control since they’re not exposed to variables such as saliva, blood, neighbouring teeth, compared to a normal composite filling.

That also means you won’t need to be with your mouth opened for too long as the majority of the work is being handled in our lab while you wait.

Designed by us, made by us

Our Crowns are now made fully in-house using digital scans and robotic milling. We only use High Quality products from from leading international companies such as Ivoclar, Dentsply and Kuraray.

3D Scans

At Proud Smile we use the latest technology in digital scanning which means we don’t take rubber moulds anymore. This makes things a lot cleaner and more comfortable for our patients and delivers unprecedented levels of accuracy and versatility. Digital scans often take only a few minutes to do and a 3D image is then reflected on our screen which is a perfect replica of your teeth and gums.

Digital Design

Once image has been acquired we then begin the process of designing your crown, all digitally on the computer screen using algorithms and off course our human eye for aesthetics.

Robotic Milling

It’s showtime! Using precision robotic technology a crown is milled out of a block of porcelain or Zirconia and it’s made to the exact specifications to fit your tooth perfectly.

Firing

The crown now goes through a firing process with temperatures up to 800 degrees. This is the process that develops the material strength and gives it a high gloss shine. The crown is ready to be cemented.
