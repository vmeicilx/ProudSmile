At Proud Smile we offer a range of orthodontic treatments to correct crowded teeth and give you a straight and healthy smile.

We offer Braces at our clinic, but it doesn’t need to be the “train track” you probably got accustomed to see. Since we are a cosmetic practice we’re very conscious of making this process as discreet as possible for our patients. With the use of Saphire brackets and white coated wires you’ll enjoy straight teeth with minimal visibility. The benefits of braces are increased predictability, 24 hour action with minimal compliance and advanced mechanics for those hard to treat cases.
