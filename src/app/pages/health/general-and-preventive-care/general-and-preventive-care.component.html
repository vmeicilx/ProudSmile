We’re great at the basics too

Even though at Proud Smile we are strong on aesthetic procedures doesn’t mean we don’t do basic procedures such as scale and cleans, white fillings, fissure sealants etc. In fact, we’re a general dental practice and we do everything under one roof on all our Gold Coast locations.

Comprehensive examination
& Check-ups

We are really Proud of our examinations and even though this is where we don’t perform any treatments we will use all our expertise and experience to talk to you and understand your requirements, fears and dreams and propose a treatment that takes into consideration your needs, wishes and one that fits your budget. We encourage our patients to be proactive and also to think of the future so during your initial examination we will discuss not only your present situation but we will also give you an insight of what to expect for the future and what we can do to minimise possible complications so you can really trust all your dental needs in our hands and we will take care of you and your family.

Our comprehensive new patient examination is, well, comprehensive. You will get:

Bite checks for any dysfunction and possible causes of wear (Orthodontic recommendation provided).

TMJ joint checks and lymph nodes to check for signs of infection.

Periodontal (gums) check.

3D scan of your mouth so we can monitor changes overtime.

Cancer screen.

Full mouth examination, teeth, gums, cheeks, tong and lips.

No maximum number of x-rays and no charge for more than 2.

Scale and Clean

We have a dedicated Dental Hygienist in our practice (Bundall location only). She will clean and polish your teeth so they’ll look brand new. She can also perform small fillings on children as well as preventative procedures such as fissure sealants and fluoride applications. Her skills also allow for deeper cleanings under local anaesthetics for patients with moderate gum disease called Periodontitis. For patients with a lot of fillings, implants or if you wear braces having regular scale and cleans is of extreme importance as bacterial will usually trap on those areas more than anywhere else in the mouth.

Our instruments will provide a gentle water spray and vibration which will penetrate even the smallest and hardest areas to reach breaking down all the plaque and calculus.
