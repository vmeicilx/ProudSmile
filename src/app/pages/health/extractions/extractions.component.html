THE OFFICE

Wisdom
Teeth

Wisdom teeth are often the cause of problems in the mouth. Lack of space causes these teeth to become impacted and with not enough room to erupt. They are usually associated with constant episodes of infection and pain due to the inability to clean them efficiently. If you have pain on a wisdom tooth it should get checked out by us. A lot of times extractions are the treatment of choice for wisdom teeth due to the recurrent nature of the problem and the lack of space for these teeth to successful stay in the mouth.

Wisdom teeth have a reputation for being difficult to be extracted however that is not all true. The upper wisdom teeth can be extremely simple to be removed. Anaesthesia for these teeth is virtually painless and the removal itself is quite speedy, usually just a few minutes. A lot of the times you won’t even notice the procedure is done. The lower wisdom teeth tend to be a little more difficult but the procedure is still pain free.

When wisdom teeth are considered more difficult to be removed, such as when they’re still inside the gums or severely impacted we usually refer these to Oral Surgeons who are specialists in teeth extractions.
