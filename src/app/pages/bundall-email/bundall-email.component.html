A New Proud Smile Dental




Dear Proud Smile patient, we're approaching our 10th year anniversary. Yes, we can't believe it either! So, it's time for us to refresh our brand to better reflect our growth, our improvements and who we are. 

In the last couple of years we've been working hard on a new logo, new colour scheme, brand new website and we've made several improvements in our treatments with the introduction of digital technology. 

We're now called Proud Smile Dental and Aesthetics. A modern approach to dentistry focused on your experience, in the minimal of the details. A decor that is inspiring, fashionable, tasteful and warm, a place where you will actually want to visit. 

We continue to be a clinic of firsts on the Gold Coast and we now offer treatments which are 100% digital, including complex aesthetic services such as porcelain veneers which can now be done in a single day, rather than weeks, we're now leaders in this technology. 

We have recently added a new location to our family and our flagship clinic in Bundall will now undergo a refresh and expansion as we will now occupy the entire building so that means more rooms, more FaceSpa Facial Aesthetics and a new surgical room with Twilight Sedation will be available soon. 

We hope you get impressed by our new visuals and we invite you to take a look at our amazing new website for more information, there's a link on the bottom of the e-mail. You can also follow us on Instagram to see what we're up to @proudsmile_dental.
