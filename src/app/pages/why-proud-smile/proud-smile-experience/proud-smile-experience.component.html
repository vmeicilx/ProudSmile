Dentistry Reinvented

Proud Smile is a purposely built dental practice with one idea in mind: to change the face of dentistry. For decades dentistry has been associated with pain, anxiety, bad smells, cold, too expensive and judgmental. We decided it was time to change. Using proven elements from other types of businesses we transformed our practice into a place where you’ll actually want to visit.

Virtually pain free

There’s no doubt that pain is one of our biggest fears and unfortunately a lot of our patients carry trauma from past experiences. However, with modern techniques and advancements in local anaesthetics we can comfortably say treatments are now virtually pain free. Depending on the procedure and the location in the mouth chances are you won’t even feel the application of local anaesthetics, you really will be surprised.

Plus, with the introduction of digital dentistry we can now perform some procedures with no anaesthetics, such as our Ultra Thin Veneers.

New Patient?
Welcome to our
family.

Our comprehensive new patient examination is, well, comprehensive. You will get:

Bite checks for any dysfunction and possible causes of wear (Orthodontic recommendation provided).

TMJ joint checks and lymph nodes to check for signs of infection.

Periodontal (gums) check.

3D scan of your mouth so we can monitor changes overtime.

Cancer screen.

Full mouth examination, teeth, gums, cheeks, tong and lips.

No maximum number of x-rays and no charge for more than 2.

You Can Afford It

We believe that good quality dentistry, whether for health or aesthetics, should be accessible to all. Our prices are quite transparent and you will always know the cost before any procedure so there’s no nasty surprises.

We offer a comprehensive range of payment plans, including our own Smileasy Payment plan. No deposit, no credit assessment and no interest. We also accept all health funds and we are preferred providers for HCF, Bupa, Medibank Private, CBHS and NIB First Choice (*).

Our treatment plans are personalised and we can tailor them to your budget so chances are we will have a solution for you.

(*)Medibank Private members choice available at Pacific Fair only and NIB First Choice and CBHS available at Bundall only.

We got you
covered

We know dentistry is not an exact science and as much as we follow strict protocols sometimes things can go wrong. Fortunately these are rare and we understand the impact it can have on our patients, which is why we offer a warranty on a number of procedures to make those unlikely events less stressful to our clients.

You can be at ease knowing we will be with you in the good and bad times. All we ask is that you see us regularly for your check ups so we can identify problems early.
