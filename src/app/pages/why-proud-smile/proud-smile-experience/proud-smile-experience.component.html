Virtually pain free

There’s no doubt that pain is one of our biggest fears and unfortunately a lot of our patients carry traumas from past old experiences. However, with modern techniques and advancements in local anaesthetics we can comfortably say treatments are now virtually pain free. Depending on the procedure and the location in the mouth chances are you won’t even feel the application of local anaesthetics, you really will be surprised.

Plus, with the introduction of digital dentistry we can now perform some procedures with no need for anaesthetics, such as our Ultra Thin Veneers.

Our comprehensive new patient examination is, well, comprehensive. You will get:

Bite checks for any dysfunction and possible causes of wear (Orthodontic recommendation provided).

TMJ joint checks and lymph nodes to check for signs of infection.

Periodontal (gums) check.

3D scan of your mouth so we can monitor changes overtime.

Cancer screen.

Full mouth examination, teeth, gums, cheeks, tong and lips.

No maximum number of x-rays and no charge for more than 2.

You Can Afford It

We believe that good quality dentistry, whether for health of aesthetics, should be accessible to all. Our prices are quite transparent and standard and you’ll always know the cost before any procedure so there’s no nasty surprises.

We offer a comprehensive range of payment plans, including our own Smileasy Payment plan(*) which has no credit checks and no need to prove your earnings. We also accept all health funds and we’re preferred providers for HCF, Bupa, Medibank Privateand CBHS.

Our treatment plans are very personalised and we can tailor it to your budget so chances are we will have a solution for you and we’ll work with your budget.

We got you
covered

We know Dentistry is not an exact science and as much as we follow strict protocols sometimes things can go wrong. Fortunately these are rare and we understand the impact it can have on our patients, which is why we, as a practice, offer assistance on a number of procedures to make those unlikely events less stressful to our clients. For example, if we place an implant and it gets rejected you’ll get a new one at no extra cost.

You can be at ease knowing we’ll be with you in the good and bad times. All we ask is that you see us regularly for your check ups so we can identify problems early.
