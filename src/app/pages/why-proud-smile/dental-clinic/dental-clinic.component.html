Welcome, you are
our guest

Our lounge is intimate and comfortable so you’ll feel right at home. Our front office team is not hiding behind a high desk and you won’t see piles of paper or archives, we’re a clean and paperless practice, no visual pollution here. Check ins are fully digital and synchronised and if you’ve filled out our medical form from home you won’t have to do a thing, just relax.

Most likely you won’t have to wait too much but if you do take advantage of a chilled glass of water or make yourself at home and grab a chilled lemonade from our minibar. Or better yet, just warm up to a freshly ground Zaraffas coffee from our machine. You’ll almost wish we run behind.

Relax, you are at a
Proud Smile Dental

At Proud Smile relaxation is taken seriously as we understand a lot of our patients are usually in fear or nervous. The famous dentist smell was replaced by beautiful scents throughout the whole clinic, usually found on department stores.


Sophisticated lounge music will put you at ease and natural light from all our rooms will feel refreshing (Bundall only).


Large TV`s in our ceiling just above our treatment chairs means you can watch Netflix and your favourite shows laying back while we work on your teeth. We provide noise cancelling headphones which will quite the sounds around you. Only thing we can`t do is popcorn.


Exclusively to our clinic we`ve installed coloured LED lighting in our treatment rooms. You`ll probably have seen these in airplanes and research shows that our mood is greatly influenced by certain lighting. As a general rule we are more relaxed when there`s less light so our whole practice has dimmed lighting throughout. Shades of purple or pink will set the mood for relaxation for when we`re treating you and blue or green feels refreshing at the end of your treatment. A warm towel to refresh your face just adds the final touch.

Technology that comforts

Technology is often associated with things non human but for us here we believe in using it to our advantage to increase focus on patient care.

Our recent investment in digital technology could easily be considered a water divider between old and new dentistry and it has changed the way we do dentistry forever.

Instead of the old style rubber moulds we can now scan your teeth using state of the art 3D scanners and then from there we can produce a myriad of different types of restorations and appliances with nearly 100% precision and completely outside the mouth. Less work inside the mouth means new heights of comfort, never seen before. You’ll be truly amazed at what we can do but most importantly at how little we need to do in your mouth.
