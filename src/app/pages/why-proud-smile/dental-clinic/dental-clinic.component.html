THE OFFICE

Relax, you’re at a
Proud Smile Dental

At Proud Smile relaxation is taken very seriously as we understand a lot of our patients are usually in fear or nervous. The famous dentist smell was replaced by beautiful scents throughout the whole clinic, usually found on department stores.

Sophisticated lounge music will put you at ease and natural light from all our rooms will feel refreshing.

Large TV’s in our ceiling just above our treatment chairs means you can watch Netflix and your favourite shows laying back while we work on your teeth. We provide noise cancelling headphones which will quiet the sounds around you. Only thing we can’t do is popcorn.

Exclusively to our clinic we’ve installed coloured LED lighting in our treatment rooms. You’ll probably have seen these in airplanes and research shows that our mood is greatly influenced by certain lighting. As a general rule we are more relaxed when there’s less light so our whole practice has dimmed lighting throughout. Shades of purple or pink will set the mood for relaxation for when we’re treating you and blue or green feels refreshing at the end of your treatment. A warm towel to refresh your face just adds the final touch.

MORE TECHNIQUES

Technology that comforts

Technology is often associated with things non human but for us here we believe in using it to our advantage to increase focus on patient care.

Our recent investment in digital technology could easily be considered a water divider between old and new dentistry and it has changed the way we do dentistry forever.

Instead of the old style rubber moulds we can now scan your teeth using state of the art 3D scanners and then from there we can produce a myriad of different types of restorations and appliances with nearly 100% precision and completely outside the mouth. Less work inside the mouth means new heights of comfort, never seen before.

You’ll be truly amazed at what we can do but most importantly at how little we need to do in your mouth.
