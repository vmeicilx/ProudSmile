Your Dream Smile
in just one day

The future of cosmetic dentistry has arrived at Proud Smile. With 3D technology now a reality we can assess, design and produce porcelain crowns and veneers in a single day procedure** with a totally digital workflow and with near 100% precision.

* For Ultra Thin Veneers, ** Case dependant

"The" day

With the use of Robotic milling we’re now able to produce state of the art porcelain restorations right in our office. Production time is cut from weeks to a single day. And don’t think for a moment that the machines took over, the design is still completely human made, only it’s done in the digital world.

Photography, Teeth prepatation (unless UltraThin), Digital Scan

We start our Same Day Veneer service with professional photography in our studio. Our dentists will discuss your specific requirements and also present you with our recommendation. We’ll explain the whole logistics of the day so you know exactly where you stand. Most of the times you’ll have already attended our Free Smile Assessment preceding this day and at this time we’ll be able to present you with a Digital Smile Proposition for your consideration. At Proud Smile we do things differently, we start with the final outcome in mind and work relentlessly to deliver it. For you it means predictability and being in control of what you’re getting. Your teeth will get “prepped” at this part of the process, unless you’re eligible for our Ultra Thin Veneers, in which case minimal or no prep is required and finally digital scans will be taken, no goopy moulds here.

Design and Manufacturing, while you relax at home

While you relax at home for the day our work at the practice intensifies. We will design your veneers 100% digitally using state of the art software and taking into consideration all that was discussed previously with you. We use “golden proportions” tools to get that perfect and natural smile designed. In our in-house lab we then manufacture your veneers using high end robotic milling machines which will cut the veneers out of premium porcelain blocks (or any other material of your choice). The last step needs some human artistic to bring character and brilliance and finally the veneers go into a furnace with temperatures of up to 1500 degrees Celsius.

And if you just can’t stop thinking about what we’re doing while you’re at home waiting we cater for you too. Simply download our Smilecloud Passport app on your phone and get access to your photos, design propositions and even updates on how we’re going with your case. And no we don’t have a 24 hour camera for you to peek, yet. ☺️

Cementation,
 Proud Smile Delivered

On your return to our practice we’ll place the veneers on your teeth and allow you to get wowed by them before we proceed with cementation. Yes, you will have the chance to look at them in your own mouth before we proceed. You then give us the go ahead and while you dry up your tears and we’ll prepare them for final cementation. It’s a new you in just a day!
