A stunning smile is no longer only for genetically blessed individuals, With porcelain veneers anyone(*) can now have a smile to be proud of. Veneers are thin porcelain "shells" that are "bonded" to the front of your teeth and can permanently change the shape and colour of your teeth to your exact requirements. Veneers can achieve the smile you have always dreamed of and one that you deserve.

Delicate yet
Strong

Even though veneers can be as thin as 0.3mm (Ultra Thin Veneers) once bonded to teeth enamel they become extremely strong. We only use premium ceramics with strengths ranging from 250mpa up to 850Mpa, depending on indication and budget. In other words, you'd need at least a 250 pound force inflicted on them before they'd break. Veneers are made to survive the harsh mouth environment which has large variances of temperature, cidity, bacterial attachment and the tough forces of the bite. It's important that your bite is properly assessed, as well as your diet, habits and lifestyle so we can increase the longevity of these types of restorations.

Digital Veneers:
Art meets maths

3D Digital Technology has revolutionized Dentistry forever. Goopy moulds, stone models and manual porcelain layering are replaced with digital impressions, 3D printed models and milled ceramics using robotic technology. Porcelain veneers can now be produced in-house with near 100% precision and in just hours instead of weeks.

End of a goopy
moulds

Hate the gagging feel of taking rubber impressions? Fear no more! Your teeth can now be scanned using a camera that creates a 3D image of your teeth. No mess, no gagging, no repeats. Images can then be 3D printed or used to create a myriad of restorations and appliances.

Smile Design

Once your teeth are scanned, it's time to design your veneers. Wax and manual brushes are replaced with state-of-the-art design software. But don't worry, the principles of teeth design are still very human. At Proud Smile all our designs are done in house by our dentists so you get a personalised smile made exclusively for you and to the minimal details and specifications.

The make of
veneer

It's milling time! Based on the 3D design the Veneers are cut out of a porcelain block using robotic technology which clones the design. They're then polished and coated with a glaze material before being fired at a temperature of 700c. Veneers then come out strong and glossy ready to be cemented.
