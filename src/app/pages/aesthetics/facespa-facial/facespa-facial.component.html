Not just smiles

Proud Smile launches FaceSpa Dermal Aesthetics, a special section of our clinic dedicated to the care and rejuvenation of the face.

Dentistry is rapidly evolving to include aesthetic treatments for the face such as muscle relaxants and dermal fillers but at Proud Smile we went a step further and we transformed one of our rooms into a full scope Dermal Spa Clinic offering treatments such as facials, peels, skin needling, PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma), Light Therapy, Microdermabrasion and more so we really are now a one stop shop for all things relating to your face and mouth.

In our constant strive for innovation and bespoke quality aesthetic treatments we developed a concept of personalised skin propositions where you will experience different levels of treatment depending on your desired results, preferred smells and textures and also your budget.

Lais and our other beautiful dermal therapists have built a reputable experience in the industry after having worked in other skin clinics on the Gold Coast and they have now embraced our philosophy of delivering individualised, high quality treatments in a friendly and personal environment.
