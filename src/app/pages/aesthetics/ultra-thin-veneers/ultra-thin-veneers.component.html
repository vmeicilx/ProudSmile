Be Proud of yourself!

A stunning smile is no longer only for the genetically blessed individuals. With porcelain veneers anyone(*) can now have a smile to be proud of. Veneers are thin porcelain “shells” that are “bonded” to the front of your teeth and can permanently change the shape and colour of your teeth to your exact requirements. Veneers can give you the smile that you always dreamed of and one that you deserve.

Science mimiks Nature!

Ceramics are the material of choice when it comes to mimicking teeth enamel due to its similarities of light properties and a variety of beautiful colours and translucencies. We also imitate the shape and texture of natural teeth onto the veneers for either that perfectly imperfect natural smile or just pure perfection, whatever you decide.

* this treatment is not suitable for all patients and we as health professionals have an ethic to let you know whether you are a candidate, a comprehensive examination must be performed to check eligibility and to assess the health of your teeth prior to receiving this treatment.

Delicate yet Strong!

Even though veneers can be as thin as 0.3mm(Ultra Thin Veneers) once bonded to teeth enamel they become extremely strong. We only use premium ceramics with strengths ranging from 250mpa up to 850Mpa, depending on indication and budget. In other words, you’d need at least a 250 pound force inflicted on them before they’d break. Veneers are made to survive the harsh mouth environment which has large variances of temperature, acidity, bacterial attach and the tough forces of the bite. It’s important that your bite is properly assessed, as well as your diet, habits and lifestyle so we can increase the longevity of these types of restorations.

Digital Veneers: Art meets Maths (measured)!

3D Digital Technology has revolutionised Dentistry forever. Goopy moulds, stone models and manual porcelain layering are replaced with digital impressions, 3D printed models and milled ceramics using robotic technology. Porcelain veneers can now be produced in-house with near 100% precision and in just hours instead of weeks.

The end of Goopy Moulds!

Hate the gaging feel of taking rubber impressions? Fear no more! Your teeth can now be scanned using a camera that creates a 3D image of your teeth. No mess, no gaging, no repeats. Images can then be 3D printed or used to create a myriad of restorations and appliances.

Smile Design!

Once your teeth are scanned, it’s time to design your veneers. Wax and manual brushers are replaced with state-of-the-art design software. But don’t worry, the principles of teeth design are still very human. At Proud Smile all our designs are done in house by our dentists so you get a personalised smile made exclusively for you and to the minimal details and specifications.

The make of a veneer!

It’s milling time! Based on the 3D design the Veneers are cut out of a porcelain block using robotic technology which clones the design.

They’re then polished and coated with a glaze material before being fired at a temperature of 700c. Veneers then come out strong and glossy ready to be cemented.
