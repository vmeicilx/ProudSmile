Get everything,
sacrifice nothing

Painless, no
anasthesia required

Since these are so thin, much less preparation need to be done on the teeth to receive them, as a matter of fact in some cases teeth require no preparation or trimming whatsoever, meaning no local anaesthetics, no pain and procedure is reversible. Preparation procedure usually takes less than an hour and involves digital scans only. Cementation then happens either in the afternoon (Same Day Veneers) or on another day as there's no need for temporary veneers.

* this treatment is not suitable to all patients and we as health professionals have an ethic to let you know whether you are a candidate. A comprehensive examination must be performed to check eligibility and to assess the health of your teeth prior to receiving this treatment.

What can Ultra
Thin Veneers do?

Ultra thin veneers are best used in cases requiring augmentation or additive cases. These are:

Permanent White
Teeth, on your terms.

Who doesn't love a fresh white smile? Even though whitening treatments such as our Zoom can be effective to lighten the colour of your teeth they require maintenance with home care. Plus, results are variable from person to person. For a permanent and predictable whitening solution ultra thin veneers is your best option, and there's no regular maintenance required, no gel, no trays. Because these veneers are so thin they are particularly good if you already like your teeth or perhaps you have just finished your braces or Invisalign and you just would love your teeth to be permanently whiter for that final touch, without necessarily having to change the shape or anything else about your teeth.
