You can Afford it

Affordability is very important for us at Proud Smile. We understand that paying for treatments in full and upfront is not always possible for a lot of people so we designed an extensive range of payment plan solutions to make sure everyone can access our premium and bespoke dentistry.


When it comes to payment plans no other practice on the Gold Coast is more flexible than us. Our Treatment Plan coordinators will always work with you to tailor a plan that works for you and your budget, whatever that may be.

Smileasy features a collection of various different payment solutions where we’ll match the best possible payment solution to your specific needs, whether it is a no deposit or low deposit plan, short or long term, minimal or no paperwork, you’re guaranteed to find a solution that best fits your situation, we do all the work for you.

Once the payment plan is in place treatment starts immediately and there’s no more financial transactions to make, we can just concentrate on getting your oral health or aesthetics to its best shape.
