What's Next

You will receive an SMS reminder 2 days prior to your appointment, please confirm with YES once you receive it. Unconfirmed appointments cannot be guaranteed and may be subject to cancelation.

You may receive an invitation to fill out our medical history form from home so you can save time at the practice. This is usually an e-mail and a code via SMS which you’ll use on the e-mail form. Please check your mail box or junk mail for instructions on how to do this.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards and most health funds. Please remember to bring your health insurance card for on the spot claiming, that way you’ll only have to pay the gap. We also do bulk billing for Medicare Child Benefit Scheme and Department of Veteran Affairs (DVA).
