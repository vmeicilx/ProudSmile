Your dentures, anchored

Dentures have long been the choice of rehabilitation for patients with no teeth, however they are believed toreduce the eating ability by 80% compared to having natural teeth. That is because they rely solely on the gums for mechanical retention and stability, making them prone to slipping and getting out of place.

What if we could substantially increase stability on your dentures with as little as 2 implants? With Dental Implants we can increase stability of your denture by creating “attachments" on the inner part of the denture and these will then create friction to the head of the implants placed in the jaw bone. For the lower jaw we only require 2 implants and on the upper jaw we require 4 at least.

One of the great advantages of this approach is the cost since we’re only placing implants and keeping your original denture with a simple modification.
