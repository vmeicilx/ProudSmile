Having no teeth is a
disability

A lot of us think that being disable means you are missing a leg, or an arm, however having no teeth at all is also a severe form of disability. Teeth are extremely important for our eating and speaking capabilities.

Being able to restore such an important part of our body can have an amazing impact in the lives of our patients, not only physically but also mentally.
